## 🎯 Installation in Stremio

### 📱 For Stremio Android:
1. Open **Stremio** on your Android device
2. Go to **Add-ons** → **Community Add-ons**
3. Enter this URL: `http://localhost:3000/manifest.json`
4. Tap **Install**

### 🖥️ For Stremio Desktop:
1. Open **Stremio** on your computer
2. Go to **Add-ons** → **Community Add-ons**  
3. Enter this URL: `http://localhost:3000/manifest.json`
4. Click **Install**
